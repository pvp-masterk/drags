Frequently Asked Questions

How do I join the server?

Click the "Join Server" button on our website or use our invite link: https://discord.gg/dUWFNqYfPX. You'll need a Discord account to join. Once you're in, make sure to read our rules in the #welcome channel!

How can I get a special role?

We offer several ways to get special roles:

  • Participate in community events
  • Be an active and helpful member
  • Server boosters get exclusive colors
  • Some roles are given by admins for special contributions

Check #roles-info for current role opportunities!

Where can I suggest server improvements?

We welcome all suggestions in our #suggestions channel! Please format your suggestions clearly and include as much detail as possible. Our team reviews suggestions weekly, and popular ideas often get implemented.

For major suggestions, consider creating a poll in #polls to gauge community interest.

How do I apply for staff?

Staff applications open periodically when we need new team members. Requirements include:

  • Minimum 3 months in the server
  • Good standing with no recent warnings
  • Active participation in the community

When applications are open, you'll find the form in /apply. We notify everyone when we're hiring!

Why was I muted/banned?

All moderation actions are logged with reasons. If you were muted or banned:

  1. Check your DMs for a message from our bot with details
  2. Review our rules in #rules to understand what might have happened
  3. For appeals, contact the moderator who took action or DM an owner

We're happy to explain our decisions and help you understand how to avoid issues in the future.

How do partnerships work?

Server partnerships are mutually beneficial relationships between communities. Our requirements:

  • Minimum 100 active members
  • Similar community focus/values
  • Active staff team
  • No NSFW content

Apply through our Partnerships page or DM an owner with your server details. We review applications weekly.

What are the server's premium features?

By boosting our server, you unlock:

  • Exclusive color roles
  • Special badge on your profile
  • Access to VIP channels
  • Priority in event participation
  • Custom emoji slots

Boosts also help us pay for better bots and server features for everyone!

How do I report a user or issue?

For urgent issues:

  1. Use the @Staff mention in serious situations
  2. DM any available moderator
  3. Submit a ticket in #help-desk

For non-urgent reports, please use our ticket system to keep mod DMs clear for emergencies.
