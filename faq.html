Frequently Asked Questions

How do I join the server?

Click the "Join Server" button in our website.

How can I get a special role?

Some roles are earned through participation, others are only given by owners. Boosters get special colors!

Where can I suggest server improvements?

Use the #main channel. We respect each suggestion.

How do I apply for staff?

Check our home page for application.

Why was I muted/banned?

We do not moderate anyone without a valid reason, if you think your moderation was unfair please dm one of the owner.

How do partnerships work?

See our Partnerships page for requirements. Both servers must meet member activity standards.
