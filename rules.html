Server Rules

General Conduct

Be respectful to all members - no harassment, hate speech, or discrimination of any kind
No NSFW or explicit content in non-NSFW channels (includes text, images, and links)
English only in main channels (other languages permitted in designated areas)
No spamming, flooding, or excessive pinging (@everyone/@here without permission)
Keep conversations appropriate for all ages in general channels
Violations may result in: Warning, mute, kick, or ban depending on severity and frequency

Voice Channels

No earrape, loud noises, or soundboard abuse (keep volume reasonable)
Respect speaker priority - don't constantly talk over others or interrupt
No voice changers that disrupt conversation or are excessively annoying
Follow channel-specific rules (e.g., game-specific voice channels)
No recording conversations without consent from all participants

Content Sharing

No illegal content or piracy discussion (including cracks, cheats, etc.)
Always credit original creators when sharing art, music, or other media
Self-promotion only in #self-promo channel (no DM advertising)
No malicious links, IP grabbers, or phishing attempts
Keep memes and copypastas appropriate and non-disruptive

Staff & Appeals

Respect staff decisions - argue in DMs not public channels
Ban appeals via DM to server owner only (no public complaints)
False reports will result in punishment equal to the accused violation
Staff have final say in rule interpretation and enforcement
Do not impersonate staff members or claim false authority
