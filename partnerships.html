Partnership Requirements

To qualify, your server must:
  • Have 100+ active members
  • Be at least a month old
  • Have clear rules and active moderation
  • Maintain a friendly and engaged community
  • Not compete directly with our server's focus
  • Have a similar level of quality and activity

We review each application manually and may make exceptions for exceptional communities that don't meet all requirements but show great potential.
