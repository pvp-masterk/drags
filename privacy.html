Privacy Policy

Last Updated: July 10, 2025

1. Information We Collect

When you join our Discord server, we may collect and store:

  • Your Discord username and ID
  • Messages you send in our server (for moderation purposes)
  • Any information you voluntarily provide in application forms
  • Server join/leave timestamps
  • Roles assigned to you within our server

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Moderate the server and enforce rules
  • Improve the community experience
  • Contact you regarding server matters
  • Analyze server growth and engagement
  • Personalize your experience in our community

3. Data Security

We implement reasonable security measures to protect your information, including:

  • Secure storage of collected data
  • Limited access to sensitive information
  • Regular security audits of our bots and systems
  • Encryption of sensitive data

4. Third-Party Disclosure

We do not sell or share your personal information with third parties except:

  • When required by Discord's Terms of Service
  • When necessary for legal compliance
  • With trusted service providers who assist in server operations (with strict confidentiality agreements)

5. Your Rights

You may request access to or deletion of your data by contacting server staff. You have the right to:

  • Request a copy of your stored data
  • Request deletion of your personal data
  • Opt-out of certain data collection
  • Update or correct inaccurate information

6. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy periodically. Significant changes will be announced in our Discord server.

7. Contact Us

For any privacy-related concerns, please contact our moderation team through Discord.
